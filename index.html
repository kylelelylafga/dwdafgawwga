This domain has been seized

This domain, tvd.sh, has been seized by the Federal Bureau of Investigation, Homeland Security Investigations, and the U.S. Department of Justice, pursuant to a seizure warrant issued by a United States District Court.

The domain was part of an ongoing investigation into violations of federal law, including but not limited to: conspiracy to commit computer fraud, unauthorized access to protected systems, wire fraud, and the distribution of malicious software, in violation of 18 U.S.C. §§ 1030, 1343, and related statutes.

This action was taken under the authority of civil and criminal seizure laws, including 18 U.S.C. §§ 981 and 982. Individuals involved may be subject to prosecution and asset forfeiture.

For more information or to report related criminal activity, visit www.fbi.gov
